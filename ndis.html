For participants

NDIS support that actually fits your life.

I work with self-managed and plan-managed NDIS participants across Australia. The NDIS can feel overwhelming to navigate, especially when you're already dealing with a lot. I'll work with you at your pace and make sure the support you receive actually reflects your goals.

Counselling

Therapeutic counselling

Individual counselling sessions focused on mental health, trauma, neurodivergence, and the challenges of daily life. Sessions are tailored to your goals and delivered in a way that feels safe and accessible to you. Available via telehealth or in person.

Assessments

Functional capacity assessment

A Functional Capacity Assessment looks at how your disability affects your daily life and what supports you need. I'm trained to carry out functional capacity, bio-psycho-social, adaptive living skills, and mental health assessments. These assessments can support your NDIS planning and reviews.

Access and advocacy

NDIS access and advocacy

If you're not yet on the NDIS and want to explore whether you're eligible, I can help. I can also support you to understand your plan, prepare for reviews, and advocate for the supports you need. Navigating the NDIS shouldn't have to be done alone.

Behaviour support

Behaviour support

Support to understand and address behaviours that are affecting your quality of life or relationships. I use a strengths-based, trauma-informed approach that focuses on what's driving behaviour rather than simply managing it.

Capacity building

Coaching and capacity building

Practical support to build skills, confidence, and independence in areas that matter to you, including daily living, communication, relationships, and navigating systems like education, employment, and health.

Services are billed under Therapeutic Supports (Item 15_043_0128_1_3) at the current NDIS rate, in accordance with the NDIS Pricing Arrangements and Price Limits.

Eligibility

Plan management

I work with self-managed and plan-managed participants. I'm not currently registered for NDIA-managed participants.

Billing

Line item and rate

Services are billed under Therapeutic Supports, Item 15_043_0128_1_3 at the current NDIS rate, in accordance with the NDIS Pricing Arrangements and Price Limits.

Invoicing

Plan manager liaison

Invoices are provided in a format that meets NDIS requirements. I'm happy to liaise directly with plan managers to streamline the process.

Assessments

Available assessments

I'm available for functional capacity, bio-psycho-social, adaptive living skills, and mental health assessments. If you're unsure whether an assessment is appropriate for your participant, contact me to discuss.
