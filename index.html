My approach

Lived experience meets professional practice.

01

Trauma-informed

Trauma shapes how people think, feel, and move through the world. Safety, trust, and your pace come first.

02

Neurodivergent-affirming

All neurotypes are welcome. Sessions are adapted to your communication style, sensory needs, and strengths.

03

Strengths-based

People are the experts on their own lives. The role of the practitioner is to walk alongside, not to direct.

04

Social justice grounded

A strong commitment to human rights, equity, and the structural factors that shape people's lives. Complexity, not just symptoms.

05

Lived experience

I bring my own experience of adversity and recovery into the work. I understand what it means to navigate systems and find a way through.
